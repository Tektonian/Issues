# Privacy Policy for Prompt sommelier

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data and protect your privacy.

## Data Collection and Processing

### Local Processing
- Prompt sommelier operates entirely within your browser
- All user data is processed locally by default

### Anonymous Analytics 

**We don't collect any metrics**

### LLM Provider Interactions
When using prompt generation, web page data (title and url only) is sent directly to LLM provider. This is necessary for AI functionality. Your data privacy is subject to your LLM provider's policies.

We may update this policy periodically. Please review it regularly for changes.
